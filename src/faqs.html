FAQs

Find answers to some of the most common questions

  • What is EDU Connect?

    EDU Connect is an online platform designed to improve access to quality education for students in underprivileged and remote areas.

  • How can I register for a course?

    You can register by clicking on the "Register" link at the top of the page and filling out the registration form. Once registered, you can enroll in courses from the "Courses" page.

  • What courses are available on EDU Connect?

    We offer a variety of courses including Basic English Language, Introduction to Web Development, Basic Data-Structure and Algorithm, and Advanced Mathematics.

  • Is there a cost to join EDU Connect?

    No, joining EDU Connect is completely free.

  • How can I access the course materials?

    Once you are enrolled in a course, you can access all course materials from your dashboard under the "Courses" section.

  • What support is available for students?

    EDU Connect provides support through our online help center, community forums, and direct contact with course instructors.

  • Can I earn certificates through EDU Connect?

    Yes, many of our courses offer certificates upon successful completion. These certificates can be added to your resume or LinkedIn profile.

  • How can I apply for a bursary?

    Information about available bursaries and how to apply can be found on the "Bursary" page. You can view details and download application forms directly from there.
